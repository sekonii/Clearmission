Our Services
Who are we?

Clearmission Nigeria Limited is a leader in Large Format Printing, Digital Color Separation and General Printing. We are strongly committed to quick and quality delivery. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in variety of high quality services and products including wall murals, event graphics, trade-show displays, vehicle wraps, and window graphics. Clearmission Nigeria Limited is an indigenous company incorporated in Nigeria on 17th December 2005 under the company and Allied Matters Decree 1990. Our offices are strategically located within Lokoja City Center from where our numerous customers are promptly served with quality prints. We are a well known leader in printing, branding, advertising and publishing sector. The top of the range machinery being used and the commitment of personnel to consistent quality delivery ranks our company among leading services providers in the Industry.
